2022

regret - a feeling of sadness, repentance, or disappointment over something that has happened or been done

For this project, I was tasked with choosing a word I had a strong connection to and create a series of designs.

Regret is a feeling I hate deeply.

I hate to regret my actions. I am working towards being fully present without overthinking.

The graffiti on the STOP is a call to action, stop regretting…just be.

original scanned drawing with marker, later digitally edited

process images of making the stencil with a laser cutter

My inspiration for the other designs came from Asian-American recycling collectors and heaps of trash bags in the streets of lower Manhattan.

Don’t regret something, it was just going to be thrown away or recycled for a better purpose.

Live and learn.

Earliest Memory

One of my first attempts at using gouache, tasked with making a map for my earliest memory.

A memory of my backyard forest that is clouded with fog.

My neighborhood friend and I would build forts, bridges across the stream, and avoid thorns.

An attempt at making a map for those memories I can barely even see in my head.
